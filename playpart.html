Shiva Shankar Burugugadda acts in a play

The Master Builder

Author:Henrik Ibsen

BROVIK.

[_Rises suddenly, _as if in distress,_ from the_ table; _breathes heavily_ and laboriously_ as he comes_ forward into_ the doorway._]

No, I can't bear it much longer!

KAIA.

[_Going up_ to him._]

You are feeling very ill this evening, are you not, Uncle?

BROVIK.

Oh, I seem to get worse every day.

RAGNAR.

[_Has risen_ and advances._]

You ought to go home, father. Try to get a little sleep--

BROVIK.

[_Impatiently._]

Go to bed, I suppose? Would you have me stifled outright?

KAIA.

Then take a little walk.

RAGNAR.

Yes, do. I will come with you.

BROVIK.

[_With warmth._]

I will not go till he comes! I and determined to have it out this evening with--[_in a tone of _suppressed bitterness_]--with him--with the chief.

KAIA.

[_Anxiously._]

Oh no, uncle,--do wait awhile before doing that!

RAGNAR.

Yes, better wait, father!
